Totient groups

Totient groups are finite abelian groups of the form \(\mathbb{Z}/n\mathbb{Z}\) for some \(n \geq 1\).

Such a group is cyclic if and only if \(n=2,4,p^m,2p^m\) for some prime \(p \geq 3\) and \(m\geq 1\).

There is a generalization of this result for other Noetherian rings instead of \(\mathbb{Z}\). See here.
