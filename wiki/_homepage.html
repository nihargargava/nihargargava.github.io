Nihar Prakash Gargava

Postdoctoral researcher in Mathematics
Institut de Mathématiques d’Orsay,
Université Paris-Saclay, France




I am a mathematician living in the Paris region. I am funded by the Swiss National Science Foundation.

I study number theory and its connections to lattices, hyperbolic surfaces, error-correcting codes, sphere packings, quantum computing. I finished my PhD in 2023 supervised by Prof. Maryna Viazovska.

Before this, I was a masters student at EPFL and before that, a bachelor student at IIT Kanpur.
