Opposite

Let \(R\) be any ring. Then we denote \(R_{\mathop{\mathrm{opp}}}\) to be the division algebra with the same set of elements as \(R\) and the multiplication given by \[\begin{equation} (r_1,r_2) \mapsto r_2r_1, \forall \ r_1,r_2 \in R. \end{equation}\]
