Siegel Mean Value Theorem

The following is a theorem.

(Siegel, 1945 ) Suppose f : ℝd → ℝ is a compactly supported continuous function. Then, the following holds. $$ \int_{SL_d(\mathbb{R})/SL_d(\mathbb{Z})}^{} \left( \sum_{v \in g \mathbb{Z}^{n} \setminus \{ 0\}}^{}f(v) \right) dg = \int_{\mathbb{R}^{n}}^{} f(x) dx, $$ where the dx on the RHS is the usual Lebesgue measure on ℝd.
