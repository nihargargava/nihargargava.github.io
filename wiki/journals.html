General woes

  • Mathematics must be published in journals. I don’t really see why though. Having expensive math journals is not a precondition to having peer reviewed papers.
  • arXiv is free and open access already.

Specific journal woes

Math. Z.

  • Asked me for >2000 USD for open access.
  • Asked me 1150 USD for printing a single diagram in color.
  • Why can’t open access version be a PDF? Their web version butchered all my \(\LaTeX\) and they gave me only two days to try to correct it. While I was in some conference.

IEEE Trans. Inf. Theory

  • Asked me for >2000 USD for open access.
  • Their website is super old and slow. With the amount of money that they take per submission, they could hire an entire web-dev team in get a web-palace constructed.
  • Forced their two column format. My collaborator had to shrink the equations to fit in.
