For an integral domain \(R\), we denote \(R^*\) as the group of units, that is invertible elements in \(R\).

In number fields

For a number field \(K\), we mean units in \(K\) to actually mean \(\mathcal{O}_K^*\) where \(\mathcal{O}_K\) is the ring of integers.
